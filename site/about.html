About

Welcome to Adventure Bound Crafts! My name is Rachel. My husband Brad and I started ABC in 2018. We have two kids: a dog named Benny and a cat named Merlin. My husband and I are Christians. We have lived in Omaha, Nebraska since summer 2015.

I love crafts of all kinds. My mom and paternal grandma started teaching me various crafts when I was young, such as crocheting and sewing. Since then, I developed a particular interest in bookbinding and knitting. I love books and learning, which is why binding is so perfect! I learn new skills all the time, and I get to work with paper and books. I'm also a traveler, when Brad and I can get off work! I have visited many places in the US, and 6 other countries. I'm hoping to squeeze in a few more soon.

Please visit the Gallery page to see examples of my work. I sell my work on Etsy. If you have any questions or comments, visit the Contact Us page. We'll get back to you soon! Thank you for visiting our website.
